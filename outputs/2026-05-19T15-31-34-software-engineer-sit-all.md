# Nutrition
- **Balanced Diet**: Follow a sustainable eating plan rich in nutrients to support weight loss and energy. Consider consulting a dietitian for personalized advice.
- **Caloric Intake**: Monitor and adjust calorie intake to support weight loss goals.

# Movement
- **Start Slow**: Begin with low-impact exercises like walking or cycling for at least 150 minutes per week.
- **Non-Weight Bearing Exercises**: Consider cycling on a recumbent bike or water aerobics to reduce strain on the back.
- **Consult a Physical Therapist**: Before starting new exercises, ensure they are safe for your lower back.

# Sleep & Recovery
- **Improve Sleep Quality**: Adopt sleep hygiene practices such as limiting light exposure before bed and creating a relaxing bedtime routine.
- **Consistent Schedule**: Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep by maintaining a regular sleep schedule.

# Safety Notes
- **Exercise Caution**: Gradually increase physical activity to prevent injury, especially with existing lower back pain.
- **Dietary Balance**: Ensure dietary changes are balanced and meet nutritional needs to avoid deficiencies.
- **Sleep Adjustments**: Implement sleep hygiene changes gradually to avoid disrupting sleep patterns.

# When to See a Professional
- **Physical Therapist**: If experiencing persistent or worsening back pain.
- **Dietitian**: For personalized dietary advice and to ensure nutritional adequacy.
- **Healthcare Provider**: For any concerning symptoms or if unsure about implementing changes.
